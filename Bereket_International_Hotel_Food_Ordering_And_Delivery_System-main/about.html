About Bereket International Hotel

Welcome to Bereket International Hotel

Bereket International Hotel is a premier hospitality establishment dedicated to providing exceptional dining experiences. With a commitment to quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction, we bring the finest culinary delights directly to your doorstep.

Our online food ordering and delivery system makes it convenient for you to enjoy our delicious meals from the comfort of your home or office. We use only the freshest ingredients and prepare each dish with care and attention to detail.

Our Commitment

  • Fresh, high-quality ingredients in every dish
  • Fast and reliable delivery service
  • Secure payment through Commercial Bank of Ethiopia
  • Excellent customer service and support
  • Wide variety of delicious menu options

Delivery Policy

Delivery Areas

We deliver within Hossana,Ethiopia and surrounding areas. Delivery time typically ranges from 30-60 minutes depending on your location.

Delivery Fee

A standard delivery fee of ETB 80.00 applies to all orders. Free delivery for orders above ETB 1000.00.

Order Tracking

You will receive email notifications with your order status and estimated delivery time.

Payment

We accept payments through Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE) mobile and online banking. Payment verification is required before order confirmation.
